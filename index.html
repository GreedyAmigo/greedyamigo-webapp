Huh, what's Greedy Amigo?

Greedy Amigo lets you keep track of your lendings and loans from friends.
A lending can be about money or a thing.

Unfortunately we are currently not able to connect your lendings width other real users yet, but we are working day and night to implement this and other great new features for you.

This web application was developed by two students of the
University of Applied Sciences Upper Austria Campus Hagenberg .
To be more precise, by Simon Ammer in the backend and
Manuel Fuchs in the frontend.

If you got any trouble, feel free to contact us at support@greedy-amigo.com.
